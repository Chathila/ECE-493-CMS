# Clarification Report: UC-05 Save Paper Submission Draft

**Date**: 2026-02-05

## Questions & Answers

- Q: What level of validation is required for draft saves?  
  A: Partial drafts allowed with a minimal required subset.
- Q: What minimum fields are required to save a draft?  
  A: Title and corresponding author email.
- Q: How should the system handle saving when no changes were made?  
  A: Allow save and show confirmation.
