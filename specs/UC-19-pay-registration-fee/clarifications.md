# Clarifications: UC-19 Pay Conference Registration Fee

**Created**: 2026-02-06

## Questions and Answers

1. **Q**: What retry options should be allowed after a declined payment?
   **A**: Allow retry after correction or new payment details.
2. **Q**: What should the service-unavailable message say?
   **A**: Payment service unavailable; please try again later.
3. **Q**: What should the payment confirmation message include?
   **A**: Only that payment was successful.
