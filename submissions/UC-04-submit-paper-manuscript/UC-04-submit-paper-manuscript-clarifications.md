# Clarification Report: UC-04 Submit Paper Manuscript

**Date**: 2026-02-05

## Questions & Answers

- Q: What file formats are supported for manuscript upload?  
  A: PDF and DOCX.
- Q: What is the maximum allowed manuscript file size?  
  A: 20 MB.
- Q: How should file upload failures be handled?  
  A: Inform the author the upload failed and ask them to try again.
