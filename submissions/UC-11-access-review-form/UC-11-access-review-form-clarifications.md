# Clarifications: UC-11 Access Paper Review Form

**Created**: 2026-02-06

## Questions and Answers

1. **Q**: How should access denial be presented to the referee?
   **A**: Show an access-denied message on the form page and stop loading the form.
2. **Q**: What message should be shown when the review form cannot be retrieved?
   **A**: Show a "form unavailable" message with no additional guidance.
3. **Q**: How should access be handled when the invitation was not accepted?
   **A**: Treat as unauthorized and deny access.
