# Clarifications: UC-18 View Conference Registration Prices

**Created**: 2026-02-06

## Questions and Answers

1. **Q**: Who should be able to view registration prices?
   **A**: Guests and registered users.
2. **Q**: What should the unavailable pricing message say?
   **A**: Pricing information is currently unavailable.
3. **Q**: What should the retry-later message say?
   **A**: Unable to retrieve pricing; please try again later.
