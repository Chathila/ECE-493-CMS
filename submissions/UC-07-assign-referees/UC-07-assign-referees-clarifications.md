# Clarification Report: UC-07 Assign Referees to Submitted Papers

**Date**: 2026-02-05

## Questions & Answers

- Q: What is the minimum number of referees required per paper?  
  A: At least one referee.
- Q: Is there a maximum number of referees per paper?  
  A: No maximum.
- Q: What happens if the notification service is unavailable?  
  A: Assign referees, but show a warning that invitations were not sent.
