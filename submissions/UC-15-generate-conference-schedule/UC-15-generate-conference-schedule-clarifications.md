# Clarifications: UC-15 Generate Conference Schedule

**Created**: 2026-02-06

## Questions and Answers

1. **Q**: What is the expected scope of the scheduling algorithm?
   **A**: Any valid schedule using available rooms and time slots.
2. **Q**: Should missing-data errors identify which data is missing?
   **A**: Yes, identify missing rooms or time slots.
3. **Q**: Who should see the generated schedule immediately after generation?
   **A**: Only the administrator who generated it.
